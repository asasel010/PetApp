About kennel

The kennel at VIAPets provides short-term boarding services for pets when their owners are away. Only dogs, cats, and birds are accepted currently, with a capacity limit of 10 animals at a time due to space restrictions. The kennel charges a flexible daily rate of 20 per day. If the kennel is available please click the button and contact Bob.
